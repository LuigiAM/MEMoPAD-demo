Progress on Social Anxiety & Avoidance

Identifying Cognitive Distortions

Session focused on identifying automatic negative thoughts (ANTs) related to her generalized anxiety. We introduced the concept of cognitive distortions. Jane was able to identify frequent "catastrophizing" (e.g., "If I miss this deadline, I'll get fired") and "mind reading" (e.g., "I know my boss thinks I'm incompetent").

Homework: Use the provided thought record worksheet to log at least one ANT per day, identify the distortion, and formulate a balanced, alternative thought. This will be the focus of our next session.
